Sync Project

Product

Problem

The current Sync business faces challenges in maximizing revenue and customer satisfaction due to the absence of an effective customer profile management solution. This results in missed opportunities to retain high-tier individual users, upsell to higher tiers, and deliver personalized experiences, ultimately hindering revenue growth and customer loyalty.

Proposed Solution

The solution should have a robust customer profile management system that stores relevant information about high-tier individual users, including their subscription details, purchase history, preferences, and feedback.

Ideation

Implement an advanced account management system that allows high-tier individual users to easily manage their subscription, upgrade or downgrade their tier, view billing details, and access exclusive features or benefits associated with their tier.

Prototyping & Testing

Prototypes are early-stage, tangible representations of a product or idea, used to test and validate design concepts, functionality, and user interactions before the final product is developed

Prototype testing involves assessing the functionality, usability, and effectiveness of a preliminary product model to identify potential flaws, gather user feedback, and refine design elements before final production

MVP

Minimum Viable Product (MVP) is the most basic version of a product that includes only essential features, allowing for quick testing and validation of the concept with minimal development effort.
